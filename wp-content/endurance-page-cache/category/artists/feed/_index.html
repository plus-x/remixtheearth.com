BOOGIEMEISTER

Biography

Boogiemeister, aka BiOniC LoVe SoUnDsySteM, are the two musical projects of Adrian Blackhurst. He has been producing music since the year 2000, with over 40+ songs on his discography, 2 full length albums & 3 EPs. He has DJ’d at hundreds of gigs on four continents over the past few years & continues to spread his music far & wide, with a dedicated fan base, & rocking crowds wherever he roams! He DJs & organizes music events on Maui with his Beats Bazaar nights. Back in 2004 he co-founded Beats Without Borders monthly (global fusion dance parties in Vancouver, BC that just had 10th anniversary!) & band Delhi 2 Dublin (who have gone on to be quite a successful popular band). In November 2014 he launched “Remix The Earth”, an ongoing music, travel & video project spanning the globe. He uses the almighty laptop and plays bass, guitar, synths, melodica, & various percussion. His debut full length album “EthnosonicSUBfunk Theories” was released July 2008 – an album exploring genres such as Global Beats, Dub, Breakbeats & gypsytronica – with instruments from India, Persia, Korea, & Japan.

Boogiemeister produces & spins Global Beats, House, Future Funk, Moombahton, Electro-Swing & Gypsy Breakbeats & then some, with the focus always on FUN & making people BOOGIE with the magic of music! His previous project, Bionic Love Soundsystem, focused on the sounds of global & dubwise electronica, infusing the sacred & mystical of the East, with the block rockin’ beats of the West! Either way, get ready to DANCE! Listen to his tunes on SoundCloud (link below), including some free downloads & mixes. His most recent tracks include “Pachamama”, “Summertime” & others, to be released on upcoming Mucho Amor EP.

In 2007/08 he went on an epic globetrotting journey where he performed gigs on the beaches of Goa & Mumbai India, Amsterdam, Vienna, Salzburg & Krakow, as well as recording much of the EthnosonicSUBfunk Theories. He has shared the stages with artists such as Nickodemus, Cheb I Sabbah, Opiuo, Bassnectar, Bonobo, Afrolicious, Jef Stott, etc…and has performed at festivals up & down the West Coast & around the globe, including Vancouver Folk Festival, Seattle’s Oracle Gatherings, Forward Fest, Mystic Island Fest in Hawaii, Mystic Garden Gathering, Symbiosis, Get Freaky Burning Man, Source Gatherings, Hallowbaloo Fest, Art-Mixx festival & too many to list!

Adrian is currently focusing on new live & studio projects, and many gigs dj’ing & producing music events on Maui. In January 2011 he launched Beats Bazaar productions on Maui, teamed up with Origins Music International & together have organized 20+ shows the past few years in Hawaii, including an annual Dubapalooza Fest & Funkalicious night, hosting artists such as Gaudi, Ganga Giri, Bluetech, Mad Professor, David Starfire, Nickodemus, The Funk Hunters etc..

THIRD EYE TRIBE

Biography

Jacob Cino is a multi disciplinary artist born in Canada & currently living in the Caribbean island of Trinidad. An electronic music producer, DJ and Dancer. Creating art in all it’s frequencies, Jacob explores intersections of different genres and influences. Living in the West indies has influenced the palate of production, as well as his Canadian origin and rave history.

Producing under the name of Third Eye Tribe, Jacob has for over 20 years leaned toward electronic explorations of dub, reggae and deep bass. This production concept has led to live festival shows and collaborations with various artists, as well as sound design for film and contemporary dance. Art in all it frequencies! Speed it up , it turns into light. Slow it down, it turns to sound.

TAZ RASHID

Biography

Tazdeen Rashid (DJ TAZ RASHID) brings a high vibrational energy to any room instantly by mixing the beats and rhythms of Love, Ancient New World, House, Kirtan, Devotional, and all the cosmic sounds. His vision is to be a channel to cause the expansion of all those around by tapping into the creative wisdom of the body and allowing them to feel, let go and awaken. Taz has been producing and part of creating mindful events globally over the last few years and as a musician, he plays guitar, keyboard, Native American Flute, didgeridoo and hand percussion. He takes these instruments and connects to the Divine and what comes through has the ability to move his crowd to connect to their Divine higher selves.

Recently he has been performing and DJing at events such as Yoga Journal Conference, Wanderlust Festivals, Hanuman Fest, Arise Festival, Bhakti Fest, The Zen Cruise, Bali Spirit Fest, Global Mala, and many more high vibrational gatherings. With his expertise in producing and playing music for yoga and meditation. Taz’s mission is to help others to create inspiring music playlists in their classes through breath beat sequencing and other novel techniques that foster a sound healing classroom experience.

Taz’s yoga music workshops assists teachers to create the soundscape for their classes which enriches the yoga practice and also supports the instructor to develop their own unique sound. Solo Moderna

Biography

“Carnival of clever madness!” Dutch producer, remixer and musician, Solo Moderna is based in Tilburg, Holland. His music isn’t about purity, but it is a folky style fusion of genres, produced with today’s technology in a range from Global Bass to Electro swing to raggae.

Solo has produced and remixed a lot of different tracks on different labels. His Sonido Pouble EP for instance got an amazing response. As the name suggests, it was made with old and damaged instruments that Solo gave a second life!

His contibute on Galletas Calientes Records, Demencia Tropical brought his sound to Colombia which got major rotation and got a number one score on Junodownload in the UK with the track Cumbia Kingston. Solo Moderna’s music doesn’t sounds like anything else. He has a unique way of breathing life into the machines he tweaks as if they were creatures from another world playing with their own little toys – an imaginary circus where friendly freaks and alien performers come together.

Traveling around as a dj and musician brought him to many different places, willing to connect his music to others’ and share his global madness!

PEDRA BRANCA

Biography

Brazil’s culture is a rich mosaic of many cultures of the world. No wonder Pedra Branca band, from São Paulo, Brazil, plays ethnical instruments and electronic timbres to create Brazilian experimental music.

The live band founded in 2001, with Luciano Sallun direction and production musical bringing in a unique way a proper way of making music in the world in a contemporary and universal. With fifteen years of existence today could be seen as one of the biggest names of Brazilian “world music” and without doubt the largest group of “chillout / lounge” Brazilian. Successfully in Brazil managed to be a reference for numerous projects and national and international DJs, as well as a reference for the Brazilian audience, contributing to the gradual opening of these sections in Brazil. Conducted a tour in August 2008 in some festivals in Portugal as “Bons Sons Festival” and “Boom Festival”.

Besides the music the group often works with dance performances and interacting directly with the concepts and artistic expression, and Vj’s artistic languages and varied, and can thus say that a complete presentation of the group already becomes not only a musical but a multimedia shows. Are launching in 2009 the third album titled “Organismo Eletrônico”, and also the DVD in partnership with the Company. Dances of Diadema in the show “belief, who said?” by Ana Bottosso and musical direction by Luciano Sallun.

In 2010 they are touring with the album ” Organismo Eletrônico ” and playing to show “Crendices”, and play in Theatre Municipal of the Mexico City. Later this year was created the soundtrack for the show Ragas group of Ares aerobatics by Monica Alla and musical direction by Luciano Sallun. In 2011 release album “Radio Global”. And 2014 the band play in Le Berges Festival in Paris, France and release by Merkaba Records the album “DNA” and now in touring live.
